What is Drowsiness Detector

Driver drowsiness detection is a car safety technology which helps prevent accidents caused by the driver getting drowsy. Various studies have suggested that around 20% of all road accidents are fatigue-related, up to 50% on certain roads

Prevention

1) Get a full night of seven to eight hours of sleep before driving.
2) Avoid driving late at night.
3)Avoid driving alone.
4)On a long trip, share the driving with another passenger.
5)Pull over at a rest stop and take a nap.
